BDI-Agent research

Created2021-04-07|Updated2021-04-07
|Post View:

Definition

Beliefs (what agents think)

It is a set of facts stored in the agent’s memory or agent’s internal knowledge. For example; whenever an agent derives a predicate isSmokeDetected(‘true, GPSLocation(‘a, ‘b)), whatever the predicate that may either be true or false, it will be added to agent’s belief set.

Desires (Intending to do)

The ultimate objective of an agent is to accomplish the goal, i.e., isSmokeDetected(‘false, GPSLocation(‘a, ‘b)) – The desire is to put water to remove smoke. However, the agent’s desire may have priority to select new intentions based on the desire and the agent’s belief.

Intentions (Act of doingness)

The plans or set of actions that agents intend to follow and peruse their desires. Intentions can also be prioritized based on the selected plan to fulfill the desire.

Many Topic

First: Dynamic environment design

之前实验过程中的agent都是在不变的环境中工作的，现在我们要实现的是在动态环境中工作的agent，而此处的重点在于设计一个合理的动态环境。

Second: Multi-agent interaction

之前已经做了agent在遇到和自己一样的agent（goal和plan库相同）时，根据对方的goal和action判断对方的plan；这次是做在不知道对方的goal的情况下根据action进行plan的判断。

Third: Agents interact with people

agrnt根据人目前的状态主动避免做一些不合时宜的事情。例如，这边有一个人在睡觉，它就会避免在那块区域过多的移动，也不会制造噪音。
